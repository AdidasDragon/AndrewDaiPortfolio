Beyond the Data

My Personal Journey

Teams and fans alike are seeing a massive shift in how we understand human performance. An explosion of biometric wearables, live computer vision, and granular game-tracked data hasn't just changed how we watch sports; it is actively reshaping front-office strategies, athletic recovery, and how fans experience the game. As someone in love with this field, I don't believe technology will ever replace human intuition or reduce a player to a statistic. Rather, I see these data tools as a support system to elevate performance and enhance the user experience.

My foundation is rooted in the Data Science program at UC Berkeley, where I get to take rigorous classes such as Machine Learning (CS 189) and Probability (Data 140). Whether it's barebones Python libraries or standard linear models; I'm trained in the math and data intuition needed to understand complex mechanics in predictive modeling and analysis.

My core goals lie beyond the data and focus on supporting the athletes themselves. My goal is to influence this shift in data to protect and prolong careers. Whether I’m designing a therapeutic game interface for un-Tilted! or an athlete-facing dashboard, my approach is rooted in Human-Centered Design. I believe that great marketing and great technology share a common goal, empathy for the end-user. My focus is to build digital products and brand narratives that protect athlete longevity while making complex data accessible, intuitive, and powerful for my audiences.

My passion didn't simply start with models or software. It's rooted from my experiences as a student volunteer at Sonoma State's Seawolf Fit. Working in their adaptive exercise program, I saw firsthand how personalized movement programs could positively change a person's life. I was able to part in the process of someone gaining a sense of agency and empowermentover their own body. Through volunteer research, I realized that data can be a powerful tool for supporting individuals and creating positive change.

Outside of the lab, I am a creator and a competitor. My life still revolves around the culture of sports, gaming, and visual storytelling. From designing digital assets for game jams to coaching youth camps, I thrive on impactful creative projects that connect people. Whether the goal is to win a championship, launch a product, or engage a fanbase, I am committed to building the visual and analytical bridges that make high-stakes goals achievable.
